Guys & Dolls

A logo immortalized.

The owner of Guys & Dolls named her shop after her late father's restaurant that was prominent in the 1940's, and asked me to come up with something similar to the original logo. After a hi-res scan of the sole surviving menu with the logo on it, and lots of tracing and cleaning up edges and points, the only existing copy of his restaurant's hand-painted logo is now here to stay in vector form. Classy.
